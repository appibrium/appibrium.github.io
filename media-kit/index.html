05 / COMPLIANCE

Usage Guidelines

DO: Clear Space Allocation
Maintain at least 25% height-equivalent padding zone (clear space) around standalone icons and lockup signatures.
DON'T: Poor Contrast Placement
Never overlay the white-text signatures on light backgrounds or low-contrast containers. Toggle contrast on cards to test suitability.
DO: Keep Electric Mint Capped
Retain Electric Mint (#00E090) elements as interaction focal points. Cap its surface utilization at 10-15% of complete layout canvas.
DON'T: Squeeze or Distort Assets
Do not stretch, rotate, distort, or manipulate individual logo vectors or pixel grids. Scale layout elements proportionally.
